# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Everyday bettors who have accumulated freebet balance on one or more betting sites
and want to convert it into real, withdrawable money. They're not professional
gamblers — they know the basics but need a clear, fast tool to run the numbers
without friction.

## Product Purpose
A 3-way surebet calculator. The user inputs their total budget and the odds for
all three outcomes of a match; the tool calculates the optimal stake for each bet
to guarantee a return across all outcomes, maximizing how much of their freebet
balance converts to real money.

## Brand Personality
Matter-of-fact, clear, trustworthy.

## Anti-references
Dark neon sportsbook aesthetics (Bet365, DraftKings, FanDuel). Aggressive
promotional vibes. Flashy animations designed to manufacture excitement.

## Design Principles
1. Input first. The calculator is the product — every other element is secondary.
2. Results are confident. Show outcomes without hedging language or clutter.
3. No decoration for decoration's sake. Every visual element serves a task.
4. Light and readable over dramatic. The tool is used mid-session on a phone or
   laptop while comparing odds — clarity beats flash.
5. Trustworthy numerics. Numbers must be visually precise and scannable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA. Standard contrast and keyboard usability for everyday web users. No
specific reduced-motion or color-blindness requirements, but avoid color-only
encoding of meaning in results.
